This grouped violin plot compares how the values of danceability, energy, and tempo are distributed among the pop and rock songs selected for this demo and the resulting songs picked by Friendshipify. The value where the violin is the widest, represents a higher probability that all songs in the given genre will take on that value. Values where the violin is skinniest represents a lower probability of songs in the genre taking on that value. The leftmost violin group shows the distribution of danceability values among the two genres and the resulting songs. Pop songs (purple)
