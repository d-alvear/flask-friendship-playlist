Error 500

There was an internal error. This usually happens if Friendshipify cannot find one or more of the songs you typed in, either because it was spelled wrong or the title of the song was incorrect. Any songs or artists typed in should be an exact match and have correct spelling.

For example:

Correct: The Sound of Silence, Simon & Garfunkel

Incorrect: Sound of Silence, Simon & Garfunkel

Incorrect: The Sound of Silence, Simon and Garfinkel
