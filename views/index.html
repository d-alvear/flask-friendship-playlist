Friendshipify

A playlist generator for friends

Friendshipify helps friends with different tastes in music find songs they'll both like. Rather than manually making a playlist that's a mix of each person's favorite songs, Friendshipify analyzes the songs submitted by each friend, finds the similarities between them, and generates a playlist based on the musical similarities it found.

For each friend, fill out a field below with three songs using the following format:

Bohemian Rhapsody, Queen; Heartbreak Hotel, Elvis; Toxic, Britney Spears

using semicolons between songs and making sure your spelling is correct.
